Rex Sanders, PSHRA-SCP, SHRM-SCP

Public Sector HR and Governance Expert

With over 20 years of experience in public-sector human resources, Rex is a recognized leader in workplace culture, leadership, and strategic HR management. Before co-founding The Hive, Rex held executive Administrative and HR positions at a regional air quality agency, where he led organizational culture initiatives, DEI strategies, classification and compensation studies, and large-scale recruitment efforts. His ability to foster collaboration between agencies, employees, and stakeholders has made him a trusted voice in HR policy development.

​

As the President of the Public Sector HR Association (PSHRA) San Diego Chapter, Rex is an active thought leader in employee engagement, compensation strategies, and HR compliance. He is also a certified expert in workplace bullying prevention and a champion of inclusive leadership.
